About Us

Welcome to ImgShrink, your number one source for fast and secure image compression. We're dedicated to providing you the best online tool to reduce image file sizes without compromising on quality.

Our Mission

In today's digital world, website speed and storage are crucial. Heavy images slow down websites and take up unnecessary space. Our mission at ImgShrink (part of XizoaHub) is to make the web faster by providing a simple, free, and efficient tool that anyone can use to optimize their images for the web.

Why Choose ImgShrink?

1. 100% Privacy Focused: Unlike other tools, we do not upload your images to any server. All compression happens locally in your browser. This means your photos never leave your device.

2. Lightning Fast: Since there is no server upload time, the compression is instant.

3. Completely Free: We believe essential tools should be accessible to everyone.

Who We Are

Founded in 2025 by Priyanshu, ImgShrink has come a long way from its beginnings in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. When we first started out, our passion for "making the web faster" drove us to start this project.

We are continuously working to improve our algorithms and add new features like format conversion and bulk processing.
